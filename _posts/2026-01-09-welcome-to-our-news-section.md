We're thrilled to announce the launch of our new news section! This dedicated space will serve as your go-to source for all the latest updates, stories, and highlights from Volunteers on Wheels.

## What to Expect

In this section, you'll find:

- **Impact Stories**: Real stories from the people and communities we support
- **Event Updates**: Information about upcoming fundraising events and volunteer opportunities
- **Partnership Announcements**: News about our collaborations with supermarkets, food banks, and other organizations
- **Volunteer Spotlights**: Celebrating the amazing individuals who make our work possible
- **Operational Updates**: Important information about our services and logistics

## Our Commitment

Since our founding in 2020, we've been committed to transparency and community engagement. This news section is another step in keeping you informed about our work redistributing surplus food to those who need it most.

### By the Numbers

- **800+ tonnes** of food distributed
- **2.5 million** equivalent food bank visits supported
- **27,500 people** supported weekly
- **12x more efficient** than individual car deliveries

## Stay Connected

We encourage you to check back regularly for updates, share our stories with your networks, and get involved with our mission to eliminate food waste and hunger.

Together, we're making a real difference in the lives of thousands of people every week.
